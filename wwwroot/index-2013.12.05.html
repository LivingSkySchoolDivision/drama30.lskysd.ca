Welcome to Drama 30

"Every child is an artist. The problem is how to
remain an artist once he grows up."
Picasso

Drama is about discovering your creative self, awakening the senses, strengthening your imagination, and communicating this experience to others. You are invited to take part in the adventure. This course is a web-based resource which requires you to read, analyze, synthesize and do. Drama is active and moves from the theoretical to the experiential.

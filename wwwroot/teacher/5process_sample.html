Example: Images from "Learning to Fly",
North Battleford Comprehensive High School Collective, 2000.

Working through the collective process, students decided to explore issues around mental illness. The image of a kite was used as the overall metaphor for the play. A story eventually emerged about a young man, J, who is starting to experience breaks with reality, and the ensuing problems with friends, family and others which arise.

Students created their characters, wrote biographies for each and helped to create a latex mask for each (a description of the mask process is on the Learning to Fly web site link). The character masks represent J's distorted view of people - how he sees them now. Music students created signature music for each character and a haunting thematic score for the dreams and nightmares.

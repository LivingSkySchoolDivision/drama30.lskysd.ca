The Saskatchewan Drama Association
 1100 Angus Street
Regina, SK
S4T 1Y5
Tel: (306) 525-0151
Fax: (306) 525-6277
E-mail: sda@sk.sympatico.ca

A STF Special Subject Council, the SDA is a professional development resource to Drama educators in the province. SDA organizes school drama festivals and theatre workshops. It has a lending library for members that is an excellent source for scripts. The SDA also offers intensive workshops for play development and has a list of resource people in communities throughout the province.
